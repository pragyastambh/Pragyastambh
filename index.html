About Pragyastambh

Pragyastambh is envisioned as a long-term public charitable initiative dedicated to education, legal awareness, digital literacy, social justice, women empowerment and rural development. Our purpose is to build strong institutions that empower individuals through knowledge, compassion and service.

Our Vision

To create an enlightened, educated and self-reliant society where every individual has equal access to education, justice, dignity and opportunity.

Focus Areas

Education

Schools, coaching centres, libraries and future colleges.

Legal Aid

Village mediation, legal awareness and land rights assistance.

Women Empowerment

Skill development, health awareness and self-employment.

Digital Literacy

Computer education and technology access for rural India.

Health

Medical camps, awareness drives and rehabilitation support.

Research

Knowledge creation, publications and policy dialogue.
