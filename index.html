Welcome to Pragyastambh

Pragyastambh is a knowledge-driven public initiative dedicated to education, legal awareness, digital empowerment, research, libraries and community development. Our aim is to build an informed, self-reliant and socially responsible society through accessible knowledge and innovative public services.

The platform is being developed to provide educational resources, digital library services, student support, legal awareness, community participation and transparent digital management systems under one integrated portal.
