About Pragyastambh

Empowering Society through Knowledge, Education, Justice and Community Service.

Who We Are

Pragyastambh is a knowledge-driven public initiative committed to education, legal awareness, digital empowerment, research, libraries and community development.

Our mission is to create an informed, self-reliant and socially responsible society by providing accessible educational resources, digital services and community support.

Our Vision

To build an enlightened, educated and self-reliant society where every citizen has equal access to knowledge, justice, digital opportunities and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To promote education, legal awareness, digital governance, libraries, research, community participation and transparent public service through innovative technology.
