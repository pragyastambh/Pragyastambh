About Pragyastambh

Pragyastambh is a knowledge-driven digital initiative dedicated to empowering individuals, educational institutions, and communities through education, innovation, technology, and social service.


The word Pragyastambh combines two Sanskrit words:

  • Pragya – Wisdom, Knowledge, and Intelligence.
  • Stambh – Pillar, Strength, and Foundation.

Together, Pragyastambh represents "The Pillar of Knowledge", inspiring learning, ethical values, innovation, and positive change.
